Call for Proposals: Data Science and History: Practicing and Theorizing Data-Driven Inquiries into the Past

We're pleased to announce a call for proposals for a special issue of DHQ on data science and history (deadline for abstracts is July 15). For details see the DHQ CFP page.

Suspension of submission deadline, July 2023

In summer 2023, we are suspending our July 15 submission deadline to allow the journal to accomplish some essential updates. Submissions will resume with the October 15 deadline. All articles submitted over the summer will be considered at that point. Publication schedules and special issues will not be affected.
