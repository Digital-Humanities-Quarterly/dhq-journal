Author Support

DHQ seeks to create a supportive and inclusive environment for authors, including authors new to the field and early-career contributors. We are developing mentoring programs to provide guidance to authors including:

  • orientation workshops for those new to DHQ or new to digital humanities
  • reading draft abstracts and articles and providing initial feedback
  • assistance with English-language editing, for accepted submissions

Starting in January 2023, DHQ's peer review team is holding a series of online workshops to provide information for potential authors about DHQ's submission, review and publication process. The workshops are aimed at authors new to digital humanities, as part of our larger goal to expand DHQ's mentoring of authors even prior to submitting an article.
